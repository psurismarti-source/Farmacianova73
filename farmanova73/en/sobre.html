📍 Our story

The Health Mechanics
of La Garriga

Farmàcia Nova 73 has been serving La Garriga from number 73 of Carretera Nova for years. What began as a neighbourhood pharmacy has evolved into a high-performance vitality centre: a place you don't go when you're sick, but when you want to feel better.

We have seen generations grow in La Garriga. We have accompanied pregnancies, first steps, athletes seeking peak performance, people in active maturity... and we have learned that health is not the absence of disease. It is vitality. It is energy. It is being able to live at 100%.

That's why we decided to make the leap: to stop being a traditional pharmacy and become the tune-up workshop that La Garriga deserved.
