Goodbye Acne: The Definitive Plan for Clear Skin (Inside and Out) 🌟

Acne isn't just "a teenage thing". Many adults suffer from it due to stress, hormonal changes or an unbalanced diet. At the pharmacy we know that fighting acne requires more than just a cream; we need to treat internal inflammation and care for the external barrier.

← Blog

Here is our success protocol combining Nutritional Therapy and Dermocosmetics:

1. Treatment from the Inside (Nutritional Therapy)

If your liver or intestine are overloaded, your skin will show it with impurities. We need to "cleanse" in order to glow.

  • Ergybiol (Nutergia): A cocktail of minerals and zinc. Zinc is the number one enemy of acne: it regulates sebum and helps with healing.
  • Physiomance Detox (Therascience): If your acne is hormonal or stress-related, this product helps the liver eliminate the toxins that "dirty" your skin.

2. The Topical Routine: Cleanse, Treat and Restore

Externally, we need products that are effective but gentle with the skin. No burning it with aggressive products!

Step 1: Gentle Cleansing (CeraVe)

The foundation of everything is a good cleanse that doesn't break the skin barrier.

  • CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser: Ideal for oily skin. Thanks to its ceramides, it cleanses sebum while keeping the skin hydrated.

Step 2: Intensive Night Treatment (Segle)

This is where we do the "magic" to renew the skin and close pores.

  • Segle Glicolic Gel: A gentle chemical exfoliant that removes dead cells and acne marks.
  • Segle Serum Tinolvital: With marine retinol, ideal for treating adult acne, improving texture and evening out skin tone.

Step 3: Correcting Imperfections (Lierac)

If you're looking for a professional finish and to treat existing blemishes:

  • Lierac Sebologie: A line inspired by the facial "resurfacing" technique. Its keratolytic solution acts on clogged pores and shine.

💡 Your Pharmacist's Tip

Never forget sun protection! Acne marks turn into dark spots if exposed to the sun. Look for "Oil-Free" formulas.
