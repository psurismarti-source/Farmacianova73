Our manifesto

Your body is the place you'll live your whole life. Not your car, not your house: your body. And like any quality engine, it needs preventive maintenance, the right fuel and, from time to time, a thorough check-up.

At Farmàcia Nova 73 in La Garriga we've changed the mindset: we don't wait for you to get sick to help you. We're your high-performance vitality centre, the place you come when you want to perform better, feel great and age with Olympic dignity.

We have the science, we have the experience and yes, we have a touch of humour because health doesn't have to be boring. If you're in La Garriga and want to move from reactive medicine to conscious vitality, you've found your place at km 73.
